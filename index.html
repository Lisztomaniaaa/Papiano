PAPIANO · Beta App
Guide
1. Open it in your browser
Launch Papiano straight from the browser — no install needed.
2. Choose your mode
Start with Solo Piano, then explore multiplayer, learning, and challenge modes.
3. Play instantly
Use touch, your keyboard, or MIDI — whatever suits your device.
4. Set up your account
Sign in to save your profile and join the community. More account features are on the way.
5. Customize sound & visuals
Adjust the soundfont, sustain, reverb, falling notes, key labels, piano height, and theme.
6. Connect MIDI
Plug in an external keyboard or controller when you want a more serious setup.
7. Monitor performance
Open the compact monitor to check FPS, active voices, and render status.
About Papiano
Built for Fast Access
Papiano is a web and mobile piano game built for fast access, flexible controls, and a more visual way to play piano. Start from the browser, customize your sound and visual setup, then play with touch, keyboard, or MIDI.
Everything You Need to Play
Papiano combines browser piano, visual falling notes, flexible sound settings, and performance tools in one clean experience.
Solo Piano
Play piano directly from your browser with a mobile-first layout and instant touch control.
Custom Sound
Choose your piano sound source, then adjust sustain and reverb for your playing style.
Visual & Animation
Customize falling notes, animation color, key labels, piano height, and visual style.
Performance Monitor
Show a compact HUD for FPS, active voices, and render status during performance checks.
MIDI Support
Use a MIDI keyboard or controller for a more serious piano experience.
Account Profile
Account profile UI is prepared for profile identity, comments, and community features.

Privacy Policy
1. Account & Authentication
Papiano may use account login or offline access to provide profile display, comments, chat, and app-related features when online services are enabled.
2. Profile & App Data
Profile name, avatar, bio, play time, app preferences, comments, and interaction data may be stored locally on the device or synced through approved online services when online features are enabled.
3. Community Content
Messages, feedback, comments, and public profile interactions can be visible to other users. Do not share passwords, tokens, payment details, addresses, or other sensitive personal information in public areas.
4. Device & Performance
Papiano may use local browser or app storage to remember settings such as sound, visual preferences, volume, performance mode, and other device-specific options.
5. Development Status
Papiano may update features, account systems, and community tools as the web and APK versions improve.
Solo Piano
Solo Piano
Play piano instantly from your device — no setup, no waiting.
What it is
A full 88-key piano you can play straight from the browser. Open it and start playing right away with touch, your computer keyboard, or a connected MIDI device.
Controls
Tap or click keys to play. On PC, enable QWERTY mode to play with your keyboard. Swipe across the top strip to scroll octaves, and slide along keys for a glissando.
Sound & Visuals
Powered by a Grand Piano soundfont engine. Customize volume, sound source, and visual preferences to fit your device and taste.
Multiplayer
Multiplayer · Planned
Real-time piano interaction between users.
What it is
Play together with others in real time. Hear each other's notes live and share the same session from different devices.
Planned Features
Join or host a room, jam together in sync, and interact with friends while you play. Details may change as the feature is built.
Status
This mode is currently in development and not yet available. Stay tuned for updates.
Learn Piano
Learn Piano · Planned
Guided lessons to build your skills step by step.
What it is
A guided learning mode that walks you through playing piano — from basic notes to full songs — at your own pace.
Planned Features
Interactive lessons, follow-along note guides, and progress tracking so you can see yourself improve over time.
Status
This mode is currently in development and not yet available.
MIDI Player
MIDI Player · Planned
Play MIDI files with live piano visuals.
What it is
Load a MIDI file and watch it play back on the piano with falling-note visuals, so you can see exactly which keys are being played.
Planned Features
Import your own MIDI files, control playback speed, and follow along visually. Great for learning songs or just watching them play.
Status
This mode is currently in development and not yet available.
Omni Chord
Omni Chord · Planned
Play full chords with a single tap.
What it is
A simplified play mode where one tap triggers a complete chord, making it easy to sound great without knowing complex finger positions.
Planned Features
Tap-to-chord buttons, quick chord switching, and an easy way to build progressions or accompany yourself while you play.
Status
This mode is currently in development and not yet available.
Community Rules
Community Rules
Keep Papiano a friendly space for everyone in Chat and Multiplayer.
1. Be Respectful
Treat other users kindly. No harassment, hate speech, threats, or personal attacks.
2. No Spam
Avoid flooding chat, repeated messages, or unwanted advertising and links.
3. Keep It Clean
No explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content in public areas, names, or profiles.
4. Protect Your Privacy
Never share passwords, tokens, payment details, or personal information in public chat.
5. Play Fair
No cheating, exploiting bugs, or disrupting other users' sessions. Report issues to the developer.
