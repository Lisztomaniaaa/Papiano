Tentang Papiano
Built for Fast Access
Papiano is a web and mobile piano game built for fast access, flexible controls, and a more visual way to play piano. Start from the browser, customize your sound and visual setup, then play with touch, keyboard, or MIDI.
Everything You Need to Play
Papiano combines browser piano, visual falling notes, flexible sound settings, and performance tools in one clean experience.
Solo Piano
Play piano directly from your browser with a mobile-first layout and instant touch control.
Custom Sound
Choose your piano sound source, then adjust sustain and reverb for your playing style.
Visual & Animation
Customize falling notes, animation color, key labels, piano height, and visual style.
Performance Monitor
Show a compact HUD for FPS, active voices, and network status while testing performance.
MIDI Support
Connect a MIDI keyboard or controller for a more serious piano experience.
Account Profile
Login with Google to use profile identity, comments, and community features.

Kebijakan Privasi
1. Account & Authentication
Papiano may use Google login or guest access to provide account identity, profile display, comments, chat, and app-related features. Guest mode is provided for quick access without a full account.
2. Profile & App Data
Profile name, avatar, bio, play time, app preferences, comments, and interaction data may be stored locally on the device or synced through backend services when online features are enabled.
3. Community Content
Messages, feedback, comments, and public profile interactions can be visible to other users. Do not share passwords, tokens, payment details, addresses, or other sensitive personal information in public areas.
4. Device & Performance
Papiano may use local browser or app storage to remember settings such as sound, visual preferences, volume, performance mode, and other device-specific options.
5. Development Status
Papiano is still evolving. Features, storage methods, account systems, and community tools may be updated as the web and APK versions continue to improve.
